Delicious Steamed Rice

This recipe provides a simple method to cook perfect steamed rice, whether on the stovetop, in the oven, or in the microwave.

Ingredients

Cups

Stovetop (my default method):

  • 1 cup white rice – short, medium, or long grain, uncooked
  • 1 ½ cups water

Oven:

  • 2 cups white rice – short, medium, or long grain
  • 3 cups boiling water

Microwave:

  • 1 cup white rice – short, medium, or long grain
  • 2 cups boiling water

Instructions

Before you start:

Check rice type: This recipe is for the following white rice types: long grain, medium grain, short grain (excluding sushi rice).

DO NOT RINSE your rice.

Stovetop (my default):

  1. Bring to simmer – Place water and rice in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Once the entire surface of the water is bubbling gently, turn heat down to low and cover with a lid.
  2. Cook 13 minutes – Cook for 13 minutes. Do not stir, do not remove lid. Water should now all be absorbed – tilt to check.
  3. Rest – Remove from stove, still with lid, and leave for 10 minutes.
  4. Fluff – Remove lid, fluff with a rice paddle or fork, and serve!

Oven:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C/390°F (180°C fan forced).
  2. Use a small casserole pot about 24cm/10" wide with a lid.
  3. Place boiling water and rice in pot, cover with lid, and bake for 35 minutes.
  4. Remove from oven, and leave for 10 minutes with the lid still on. Remove lid, fluff with a rice paddle or fork, and serve!

Microwave:

  1. Place rice and boiling water in a tall microwave-proof container.
  2. Microwave on high for 12 minutes with NO LID (1100 – 1200W microwaves).
  3. Remove, cover with lid, leave for 10 minutes. Remove lid, fluff with a rice paddle or fork, and serve!
