Spiced Steamed Cabbage

This spiced steamed cabbage is healthy, delicious, and easy to make. It goes well with any meal or serve it with rice or chapati.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams cabbage or about 3 cups chopped
  • 1 tbsp oil/ghee or as needed
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
  • ½ tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 red chili broken or green chili
  • 1 sprig curry leaves (optional)
  • 1 pinch asafetida – hing
  • ¾ tsp ginger or garlic (or both)
  • ¼ tsp salt or as needed
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • ¾ tsp red chili powder or slit green chili
  • ¾ tsp garam masala or curry powder as needed

Instructions

  1. Steam Cabbage: Place chopped or shredded cabbage in a steamer basket. Sprinkle salt and turmeric. Toss it well. Steam in a steamer or pressure cooker (without whistle) until tender for 5 to 7 mins.
  2. Temper: Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard, cumin, and red chilies. When the seeds begin to splutter, add hing and curry leaves. Lower the flame. Sauté ginger garlic for 40 to 50 seconds, without burning.
  3. Stir in the red chili powder, garam masala, and more salt if required. (Taste the cabbage first).
  4. Add the steamed cabbage and sauté for 2 mins.
  5. Serve steamed cabbage on the side. It goes well with rice or chapati.

Nutrition Facts

Calories 50 cal
Carbohydrates 10g
Protein 2g
Fat 1g
